Fraser River Tunnel Project - Preparatory Construction Works

Application for a Fisheries Act Authorization for the Fraser River Tunel Project (FRTP) Preparatory Construction Works (PCW) as submitted by the Transportaion Investment Corporation (the Proponent).

Project Description

The proposed Fraser River Tunel Project (FRTP) Preparatory Construction Works (PCW), the Project, includes construction and operation of a marine terminal, and construction of an extended tug basin, and widened causeway. The proposed Project is located adjacent to the existing Roberts Bank terminals.

The Project is undergoing the British Columbia (BC) environmental assessment (EA) process under the BC Environmental Assessment Act, with a decision made by the Governor in Council to allow the Project to proceed to permitting subject to conditions on April 20, 2024. The Project's PCW FAA has been deemed complete and adequate with respect to DFO's application requirements and DFO is in the process of conducting Indigenous consultation for the FRTP PCW Fisheries Act Authorization (FAA) application.

DFO is of the view that the Project as proposed is likely to result in the death of fish by means other than fishing and the harmful alteration, disruption or destruction (HADD) of fish habitat. If issued, an FAA would allow the carrying on of works, undertakings or activities that would result in the death of fish by means other than fishing and/or HADD of fish habitat.
